Not necessarily. We work with what you have. SQL database? We connect directly. Event-based (Segment, Mixpanel)? We'll set up the right connectors.

That's fine — and common. The event tracking dictionary we deliver includes implementation guidance your dev team can follow. The dashboard works once events are flowing.

Yes. Metabase is open-source. We'll help you deploy it on your own infrastructure (Docker, Railway, or your cloud provider). No recurring analytics costs.
